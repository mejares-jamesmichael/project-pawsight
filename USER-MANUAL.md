## 1. Introduction

**PawSight** helps cat owners interpret their pet's body language, providing insights into their mood and needs. Whether you're trying to understand why your cat is wagging its tail or need quick access to an emergency vet, PawSight is here to help.

**Key Features:**
*   **Offline Library:** A comprehensive database of cat behaviors (works without internet).
*   **AI Assistant:** An intelligent chatbot to answer your specific questions (requires internet).
*   **Vet Hotline:** A directory of emergency and general veterinary clinics.

## 2. Getting Started

### Installation
PawSight is available for Android and iOS devices. Simply download and install the app from your respective app store.

### First Launch
Upon opening the app for the first time, you will land on the **Home Screen**. No account creation or login is required to access the core features of the app.

## 3. Home Screen Dashboard

The **Home Screen** is your central hub. Here's what you'll find:

*   **Greeting:** A personalized "Good Morning/Afternoon/Evening" message.
*   **Daily Purr-spective:** A new fun fact or tip about cats every day.

## 4. Feature Guides

### Behavior Library
The **Library** is an encyclopedia of cat body language. It is stored locally on your device, so you can use it even when you're offline.

**How to use:**
1.  Tap the **Library** icon in the bottom navigation bar or the "Tools" grid.
2.  **Search:** Use the search bar at the top to type keywords (e.g., "hissing", "tail").
3.  **Filter:**
    *   **Mood:** Filter by emotional state (e.g., Happy, Fearful, Aggressive).
    *   **Category:** Filter by body part (e.g., Tail, Ears, Eyes, Vocal).
4.  **Sort:** Use the sort dropdown to arrange behaviors alphabetically.
5.  **View Details:** Tap on any behavior card to read a full description, including what the behavior means and the source of the information.

### AI Assistant
The **AI Assistant** is a smart chatbot that can answer more complex or specific questions about your cat.

**Note:** This feature **requires an active internet connection**.

**How to use:**
1.  Tap the **AI Chat** card on the Home screen.
2.  Type your question in the text box at the bottom (e.g., "Why does my cat wake me up at 3 AM?") and tap the **Send** icon.
3.  You can also tap one of the **Suggestion Chips** (e.g., "What does a slow blink mean?") to ask a common question instantly.
4.  **Clear History:** To delete your chat history, tap the **Trash Can** icon in the top right corner and confirm your choice.

**Offline Mode:**
If you lose internet connection, an orange banner will appear saying "You are offline." You will not be able to send messages until you reconnect.

### Vet Hotline
The **Vet Hotline** screen provides a list of veterinary clinics and emergency services, primarily focused on the Metro Manila area.

**How to use:**
1.  Tap the **Hotline** icon in the bottom navigation bar.
2.  **Emergency Services:** Listed at the top with a red border. Look for the **24/7** badge for clinics that are open anytime.
3.  **General Clinics:** Listed below emergency services for standard check-ups.
4.  **Actions:**
    *   **Call:** Tap the **Call** button to open your phone's dialer.
    *   **Email:** Tap **Email** to open your default email app.
    *   **Location:** Tap **Location** to view the clinic on Google Maps.
    *   **Social:** Tap **Facebook** or **Instagram** to visit their social media pages.

**Important:** Always call ahead to confirm availability, especially for emergency cases.

## 5. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q: Do I need the internet to use PawSight?**
A: You can use the **Library** and view **Vet Hotline** contacts offline. However, the **AI Assistant** requires an internet connection to function.

**Q: Is the information in the Library accurate?**
A: Yes, the library content is sourced from reputable organizations (like the ASPCA or Cornell Feline Health Center) and is reviewed. You can see the source on each behavior's detail page.

**Q: Can the AI diagnosis my sick cat?**
A: **No.** The AI is an informational tool only. It cannot provide medical diagnoses. If your cat is sick or injured, please use the **Vet Hotline** feature to contact a professional veterinarian immediately.

**Q: Is my chat history saved?**
A: Your chat history is stored locally on your device for your convenience. You can clear it at any time using the "Clear History" button.

## 6. Troubleshooting

**Issue: The AI Chat isn't responding.**
*   **Solution:** Check your internet connection. Ensure you are not in "Airplane Mode" and that you have Wi-Fi or mobile data enabled. If the problem persists, try restarting the app.

**Issue: The Vet Hotline list is empty.**
*   **Solution:** This may happen if the database failed to load. Try tapping the "Retry" button if it appears. If the issue persists, check if you have given the app necessary permissions or try reinstalling.

**Issue: Clicking "Call" doesn't open my phone dialer.**
*   **Solution:** Ensure your device has a phone app installed and configured. On some tablets without cellular capabilities, this feature may not work.
